Catalan Numbers & Dyck Paths

A sequence of numbers that appear in many counting problems. C(n) gives the number of paths from the bottom-left to top-right of an n x n grid, using only steps right and up, without crossing the main diagonal. This animation shows one such path.

C(5) = 42
